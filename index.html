// Who is he

A Warrior Born Between Two Worlds

Eren Erarslan is a 15-year-old who carries the fire of Turkey and the spirit of the Netherlands in equal measure. Born with Turkish heritage and now living in the Netherlands, Eren has built a life that bridges two cultures — learning, fighting, and gaming his way through every day with relentless energy.

Whether he's on the tatami throwing opponents with crisp technique, or grinding late nights in Roblox chasing the next level, Eren approaches everything with the same fierce dedication. Blue is more than his favorite color — it's the color of his belt, his glow, his identity.

At just 15 years old, he's already a dedicated practitioner of judo, a competitive spirit, and a kid who knows exactly who he is. Friends know him as intense, funny, loyal, and completely obsessed with both judo and Roblox — sometimes in that order, sometimes not.

// The Sport

The Art of the Gentle Way

Judo is not just a sport for Eren — it's a philosophy, a discipline, and a second home. Stepping onto the tatami, he enters a world where focus, strength, and technique converge. Every training session is a battle, and every battle teaches him something new about himself.

"Judo taught me that falling is not failure — it's part of getting back up stronger. Every throw I learn, every throw I take, makes me better."

— Eren Erarslan, Judoka

🎯

Precision & Technique

Judo demands perfect timing and technique. Eren has spent years honing his throws, learning that a well-timed move beats raw strength every time. His favorite techniques include dynamic hip throws and sweeps.

🧠

Mental Discipline

The discipline judo instills carries beyond the mat. Eren brings the same calm focus he uses in competition to his schoolwork, his gaming, and his daily life. Judo has shaped how he thinks under pressure.

💪

Physical Strength

Hours of training every week have built Eren into a physically powerful young athlete. Grip strength, explosive legs, core stability — judo demands it all, and Eren delivers with every session on the mat.

🏆

Competitive Drive

Competitions are where Eren shines. The adrenaline of facing an opponent, the strategy of reading their movement, the burst of energy in a throw — this is where his training becomes something electric.

🤝

Dojo Brotherhood

Judo builds bonds like no other sport. The teammates you train with become your closest allies. Eren's dojo family is a crucial part of his life — they push each other, laugh together, and grow together.

🌊

Blue is the color of Eren's favorite things — and fittingly, he wears a blue belt with pride. Each color represents progression, and the blue belt marks a judoka who has built real, battle-tested skill.

// The Journey

From Turkey to the Netherlands

Eren's story is one of roots and growth — of carrying a heritage proudly while building something new. His Turkish background gives him warmth, family values, and a fierce competitive culture. His life in the Netherlands gives him opportunity, a world-class judo environment, and a future full of possibility.

Turkish Heritage
Roots Across the Aegean
Eren's story begins in Turkey — a country with a rich culture of sport, family, and pride. Turkish values of hospitality, toughness, and loyalty were instilled in him from a young age. These roots never leave him, no matter where in the world he stands.
The Netherlands
A New Home, A New Stage
Moving to the Netherlands opened up a world of opportunity. The Dutch judo scene is internationally recognized — it's produced Olympic champions and world-class athletes. For a young judoka like Eren, there's no better place to grow and compete. He embraced the Netherlands fully while keeping his Turkish heart.
The First Throw
Discovering Judo
The moment Eren stepped onto a tatami for the first time, something clicked. The combination of physical challenge, strategy, and the dojo's unique culture pulled him in completely. He threw himself into training — literally — and never looked back. Judo became his passion, his discipline, his identity.
The Discovery
Roblox Enters the Chat
Between training sessions, Eren discovered Roblox — and quickly fell down the rabbit hole. Blox Fruits and Attack on Titan Revolution became his go-to worlds. Gaming became the perfect balance to the physical intensity of judo: a mental space to compete, strategize, and have fun with friends.
Today — Age 15
The Story Continues
At 15, Eren is in full bloom — training hard, gaming harder, and figuring out who he wants to be. He dreams of competing at higher judo levels, of reaching the top ranks in his favorite games, and of honoring both the Turkish spirit that shaped him and the Dutch future that awaits him.
